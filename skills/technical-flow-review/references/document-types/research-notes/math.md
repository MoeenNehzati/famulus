# Research Notes: Mathematics Deviations

- Relative to the general research-notes baseline:
  - local technical focus is acceptable if the mathematical point of the note is explicit
  - the note should say whether it is recording an idea, a proof sketch, a failed route, a counterexample, or a cleaned-up argument
  - even when notation and context are shared, the note should preserve enough of the mathematical target that the reader can recover why the computation or lemma matters
- Common additional failure mode:
  - the reader cannot tell whether the note is exploratory, explanatory, or reporting a finished point
  - computations or lemmas appear without a visible statement of what they are trying to establish
