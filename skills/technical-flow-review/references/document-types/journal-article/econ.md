# Journal Article: Economics Deviations

- Relative to the general journal baseline:
  - the economic question, contribution, and place in the literature should become clear especially early
  - the introduction often benefits from a four-move structure: establish territory, review literature, establish a niche, occupy the niche
  - the paper should explain what is economically at stake, not merely what is technically true
  - the main contribution should be identifiable in the title and opening paragraphs
  - the paper should make it easy to tell whether the main gain is conceptual, theoretical, empirical, econometric, or some combination
- Common additional failure mode:
  - the paper reads like a mathematics note with economics added too late
  - the paper gives local technical novelty without making the economic relevance legible enough
