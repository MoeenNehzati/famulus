# Research Presentation: Computer Science Deviations

- Relative to the general presentation baseline:
  - the sharp problem framing and main takeaway should appear especially early
  - each slide should carry a message rather than merely a topic label
  - the talk should usually reveal quickly what system, model, algorithm, or empirical result is new and why it improves on a baseline the audience recognizes
  - slides should help an audience under time pressure recover the story from titles, visuals, and repeated takeaways
- Common additional failure mode:
  - the audience never gets a stable central claim
