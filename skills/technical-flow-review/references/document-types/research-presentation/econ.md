# Research Presentation: Economics Deviations

- Relative to the general presentation baseline:
  - the economic question, intuition, and payoff should stabilize very early
  - the audience should know quickly what the result says about the economic problem, not just what method was used
  - talks should usually privilege the economic mechanism, identifying variation, or strategic intuition over a paper-complete march through specifications or proofs
  - the audience should be able to retell the economic point of the talk after a few opening minutes, even if they cannot reproduce the details
- Common additional failure mode:
  - the talk becomes technically precise before it becomes economically legible
