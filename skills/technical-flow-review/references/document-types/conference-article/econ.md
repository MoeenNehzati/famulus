# Conference Article: Economics Deviations

- Relative to the general conference baseline:
  - the economic question and payoff should become clear especially quickly
  - the paper should reveal early what economic mechanism, empirical pattern, identification idea, or theoretical insight the reader is supposed to remember
  - the introduction should position the paper in the literature efficiently rather than reproducing a full journal-style literature survey
  - conference compression should not hide what is economically at stake or why the result would matter to economists
- Common additional failure mode:
  - weak sense of why the result matters economically
  - the paper spends scarce space on technical setup before stating the economic point
