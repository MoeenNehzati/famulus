% --- Basic \cite (already tested) ---
See \cite{lecun1989b} for the original work.

% --- \citep (parenthetical) ---
Backpropagation was applied to vision \citep{lecun1989b}.

% --- \citet (textual) ---
\citet{lecun1989b} demonstrated backpropagation on handwritten digits.

% --- \citealt (no parentheses) ---
This was shown by \citealt{lecun1989b} in 1989.

% --- \citealp (no parentheses, comma) ---
The approach of \citealp{lecun1989b} is foundational.

% --- \citeauthor (author only) ---
\citeauthor{lecun1989b} pioneered convolutional networks.

% --- \citeyear (year only) ---
The method dates back to \citeyear{lecun1989b}.

% --- Optional post-note: \citep[p.~10]{key} ---
See \citep[p.~10]{lecun1989b} for details.

% --- Optional pre- and post-note: \citep[see][p.~10]{key} ---
\citep[see][p.~10]{lecun1989b} for the derivation.

% --- Multi-key, no options ---
Both papers \citep{lecun1989,lecun1989b} are relevant.

% --- Multi-key with duplicate (dedup case) ---
\citep{lecun1989b,lecun1989} should become \citep{lecun1989}.

% --- Multi-key including unaffected entry ---
\citep{lecun1989b,hochreiter1997} — one affected, one not.

% --- Multi-key with optional args ---
\citep[see][]{lecun1989b,hochreiter1997} — optional args survive.

% --- Preprint cases ---
Transformers \citep{vaswani2017preprint} changed NLP.
\citet{vaswani2017preprint} introduced the attention mechanism.
See \citep[Sec.~3]{vaswani2017preprint} for the architecture.
\citep{vaswani2017preprint,vaswani2017} — dedup to \citep{vaswani2017}.
\citep{hochreiter1997,vaswani2017preprint} — one affected, one not.

% --- Unaffected citations: must not change ---
LSTMs \citep{hochreiter1997} remain important.
The original paper is \citet{lecun1989}.
